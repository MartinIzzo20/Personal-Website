Great White Shark

Great White Sharks can swim at over 60 kmph, and the shark from the movie Jaws is a great white. Jaws is an awesome movie. 5 stars.

Blue Shark

Blue Sharks, unlike most sharks, swim in groups. These groups are similar to wolf packs. 5 stars.
